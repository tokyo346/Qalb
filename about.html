About QALB

Our Mission

QALB is dedicated to empowering communities through sustainable development, education, and social support. We aim to improve the quality of life for vulnerable populations while promoting inclusive growth and equitable opportunities for all.

Our History

QALB was founded in 2026 by a group of community leaders with a shared vision of creating impactful social programs. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a recognized organization with multiple ongoing initiatives in education, healthcare, and environmental conservation.

Our Impact

Over the years, QALB has supported thousands of beneficiaries through scholarships, health campaigns, and community development projects. Our programs focus on empowering youth, supporting women, and fostering resilience in underprivileged communities.
