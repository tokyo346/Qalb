Eligibility & How to Apply

Program Eligibility

To participate in Qalb Organization programs, candidates should meet the following:

  • Age within program-specific range
  • Residency within the community or target area
  • Completed prerequisite forms or prior programs (if required)
  • Consent from guardians for minors
  • Commitment to attend scheduled sessions

How to Apply

Applications are accepted online or in person. Steps:

  • Download or collect the program application form
  • Fill in all required information and attach supporting documents
  • Submit via email or drop off at our office
  • Receive confirmation and program joining instructions

Why Choose Qalb?

Qalb Organization provides a supportive environment for personal and community growth:

  • Skilled program coordinators
  • Safe and inclusive learning spaces
  • Resources to enhance skills and knowledge
  • Mentorship and leadership opportunities
  • Community impact and networking
