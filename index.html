About Qalb Organization

Our History

Qalb Organization – Heart of Mercy was founded with the goal of building a trustworthy and organized system of charity. Our mission is to support vulnerable families, widows, orphans, and individuals facing hardship while maintaining dignity and transparency in every action.

Through community support, volunteer dedication, and responsible stewardship of donations, Qalb continues to expand its efforts to bring relief and hope to those who need it most.
